The Window Is Open

Three Reasons This Week Matters More Than Next Year

Federal retirement planning is time-sensitive. The decisions you make (or avoid) in the months before separation compound over 25+ years of retirement.

TSP Allocation Decisions

Every month your TSP sits in the wrong allocation — too aggressive or too conservative — costs you. A bear market in year one of retirement (sequence risk) can permanently damage a withdrawal strategy. Structuring your income floor NOW protects you.

The FERS Supplement Cliff

If you don't plan for the supplement cliff at 62, you'll burn through TSP faster than expected. Every month of delay in planning means less time to prepare for the USD 1,000-1,500/month income drop that happens on your 62nd birthday.

Social Security Timing Compounds

Each year you delay past 62 increases your benefit ~7%. The optimal claiming strategy depends on your FERS annuity, TSP income, and health. The earlier you analyze this, the more options you have to coordinate all three income sources.
