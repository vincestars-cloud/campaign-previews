Common Questions About Buying Verified Leads

Are these real homeowners or just random contacts?

Every contact is a verified homeowner with property ownership confirmed. We filter out renters, bad numbers and business addresses. You get real people who actually own the property.

How targeted can I get?

You can filter by state, county, ZIP, or combine multiple areas. Layer on demographic filters like credit score (650+), income ($60K+), home value ($200K+), home age (15+ years), and owner vs renter.

How fast is delivery?

Your CSV is delivered within 3 hours of purchase, directly to your inbox. Most orders come through faster. Same business day, guaranteed.

What fields are included in each record?

Every record includes first name, last name, address, phone, email, property square footage, household income, credit score range, and 85+ additional data points. You get everything you need to pre-qualify before you dial.

What if the data isn't accurate?

If your data doesn't match your requested market or format, we replace it immediately — no questions asked. Just flag the records and we handle the rest. Our guarantee is the proof.

Is there a contract or monthly commitment?

No contracts. No monthly fees. No lock-in. You buy when you need leads and own them forever. Simple as that.

How does the free replacement guarantee work?

Every disconnected or wrong number gets replaced at no additional charge. No threshold. No limit. No questions asked. Simply flag the records and we replace them.

Do purchased lists expire?

No. One-time purchases never expire. You have permanent access to every list you download.
