Theme 1: STALE DATA IS UNIVERSAL

Every platform suffers from data freshness issues. PropStream, BatchLeads, Salesgenie, ExactData - all get complaints about outdated records. The fundamental problem: county records, MLS data, and contact databases all update at different speeds.

Theme 2: SKIP TRACING IS THE WEAKEST LINK

Contact information (phone numbers, emails) is the #1 complaint category. Hit rates of 20-40% valid numbers are commonly reported. Users routinely skip trace outside their primary platform.

Theme 3: LOCK-IN THROUGH DATA HOSTAGE

Multiple vendors (especially BatchLeads) hold customer data hostage - cancel and lose everything. No portability, no refunds on unused credits, no data export on cancellation.

Theme 4: BILLING PRACTICES DESTROY TRUST

Auto-renewals without notice, charges after cancellation, trial-to-paid without consent, price hikes without warning. This is across ALL major vendors.

Theme 5: OVERSATURATION KILLS ROI

Everyone uses the same 3-4 platforms, pulling the same lists, mailing the same people. "Sellers are getting inundated." The data itself may be accurate but the competitive advantage is zero.

Theme 6: NO QUALITY GUARANTEES

Most vendors refuse to replace or refund bad data. Users bear 100% of the risk for data quality. The vendors that DO offer guarantees (like LeadsPlease's 95%+ deliverability) stand out dramatically.

Theme 7: VOLUME BUYERS HAVE UNIQUE PAIN

Duplicate management, bulk pricing pressure, need for data freshness at scale, integration/export limitations, and the inability to deduplicate across months of pulls.
