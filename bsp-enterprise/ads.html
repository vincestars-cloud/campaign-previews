Vince's Feedback Summary

Core Mistake to Fix

  • Old ads felt like "here's better data" — new ads must feel like "you're losing money RIGHT NOW"
  • Ads were too long, too educational, tried to close the sale instead of getting the click
  • Hook was buried — setup > context > hook. Must be reversed: hook FIRST

New Rules

  • 3-5 lines MAX — these are cold traffic Meta ads, not sales letters
  • ONE job per ad: hook OR pain OR shift OR curiosity
  • Open the loop, don't close it — leave curiosity, get the CLICK
  • Identity targeting: "If you're running a 5-20 rep call center…"
  • Outcome framing: they want pipeline, predictability, control — not "better data"
  • Weaponize the pain — make them FEEL vendor betrayal, not just mention it
  • B2B emotion: fear of wasting money, looking bad to boss, reps quitting

V6.1 Feedback (Round 2)

  • More BLAME SHIFT — "It's not your reps / script / offer" unlocks clicks in B2B
  • More PIPELINE CONTROL — "You don't have a pipeline. You have a subscription."
  • More TEAM PAIN — "Your reps are dialing ghosts" / "This is why your team is frustrated"
  • More TIME PRESSURE — "right now", "today", "every hour"
  • More IDENTITY HOOKS — "If you're spending $10K/month on leads…"
  • TONE: controlled intensity, economic pressure, real-world frustration — NOT hype/drama
  • CUT: soft hooks ("you might be…"), feature-heavy ads (fields/filters/platform), over-explained ads
  • Balance: confront the problem, protect the person — "You're solving the wrong problem" not "You're doing everything wrong"
