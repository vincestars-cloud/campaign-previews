Why Jeremy Chose This Angle

"In the post-Andromeda era, demonstration content creates the strongest signal for Meta's algorithm. The Dial Test converts skepticism into a testable claim. The buyer does not have to trust our copy or math. They just dial 10 numbers and count how many ring. That is a demonstration — and demonstrations outperform every other format when the product can be shown in action. For pixel conditioning, the free trial / 10-dial test creates a low-barrier conversion that trains the pixel on data-quality-conscious buyers. Anyone who creates an account, builds a list, and tests phone numbers is a serious data buyer."
