Visual System + Formula Key

5 Visual Formats

A: Headliner — Headline 40-50% canvas. Supporting text. Simple visual. CTA bar.

B: Benefit Callout — Mockup center. 4-5 benefit pills with arrows. Social proof strip.

C: Contrarian Reframe — MASSIVE headline (50%+). Minimal imagery. Copy IS the creative.

D: Objection Handler — Product center. 3 objection labels with arrows. Block color.

E: Social Proof — Fake review/comment screenshot. Star rating. Native feel.

Color Palette

Brand Dark + Dark Gray backgrounds | White text | Brand Blue accent/CTA | Fonts: DM Serif Display + Inter

Exceptions: Green for guarantee/trust, Red for TCPA/fear — intentional, not default

Every Static Gets

HomeLeads Pro wordmark + "Replacement Guarantee | 18+ Data Points" strip + homeleadspro.com CTA

Typography System (referenced in all prompts)

Headlines (H1)

DM Serif Display, 800 weight, 100-160pt (scales with canvas %). Line-height: 0.85-0.9. Tracking: -0.01em to -0.02em. Uppercase.

Subhead / Reframe (H2)

Inter, 600-700 weight, 44-60pt. Line-height: 1.1. Normal tracking. Sentence case or uppercase.

Eyebrow Labels

Inter, 600 weight, 16-18pt, uppercase, tracking 0.12em. Top of canvas, 40-60px from top edge, 60px side margins.

Supporting / Body

Inter, 400 weight, 22-28pt. Line-height: 1.4. Max 60% canvas width. 20px below headline.

CTA Buttons

Inter, 700 weight, 20-22pt. White on blue (#2563eb) rounded pill, 180x44px, radius 22px. Bottom 15% of canvas, centered.

Social Proof Strip / Brand

Strip: Inter, 500 weight, 13-14pt, uppercase, tracking 0.08em, 36px tall bar. Brand wordmark: Inter, 700 weight, 16-18pt, bottom corner, 40px margin.

Canvas margins: 60px sides, 40-60px top/bottom. Benefit pills: Inter 600, 16-18pt, rounded 20px, 8px/16px padding. Carousel cards: Card 1 scroll-stop headline 50% of card, Cards 2-4 header 36-48pt / body 20-24pt. Video: hook text 90-120pt word-by-word reveal, stats 72-96pt counter animation.
