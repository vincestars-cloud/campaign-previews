Visual System + Formula Key

5 Visual Formats

A: Headliner — Headline 40-50% canvas. Supporting text. Simple visual. CTA bar.

B: Benefit Callout — Mockup center. 4-5 benefit pills with arrows. Social proof strip.

C: Contrarian Reframe — MASSIVE headline (50%+). Minimal imagery. Copy IS the creative.

D: Objection Handler — Product center. 3 objection labels with arrows. Block color.

E: Social Proof — Fake review/comment screenshot. Star rating. Native feel.
