Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or purchase lead lists through BookedSolid Pro, we collect:

  • Account information: Name, email address, company name, phone number, and billing address.
  • Payment information: Credit card or payment method details, processed securely through our third-party payment processor. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, search queries, list builds, and download activity.
  • Device and log data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and access timestamps.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our lead list platform and services.
  • To process transactions and deliver purchased data files.
  • To send order confirmations, service updates, and support communications.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Data We Provide to You

BookedSolid Pro provides homeowner and property data compiled from publicly available records, proprietary databases, and licensed data partners. This data is intended solely for lawful business purposes including direct marketing, sales prospecting, and market research.

We do not maintain or provide:

  • Social Security numbers or Driver's License numbers
  • Credit card or bank account information of data subjects
  • FICO scores (we provide estimated credit score ranges, not actual scores)
  • Health or medical data
  • Data on minors (all records are adults 18+)

Our data practices comply with DMA guidelines, FCRA regulations, and applicable federal and state privacy laws.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal account information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Payment processors, hosting providers, and analytics services that help us operate the platform.
  • Legal compliance: When required by law, subpoena, or legal process.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), secure server infrastructure, access controls, and regular security audits. While no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable precautions to protect your account information.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session, remember preferences, and analyze platform usage. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may limit certain platform features.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about your account.
  • Request correction or deletion of your account data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Request data portability of your account information.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@bookedsolidpro.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Order history and transaction records are retained for accounting and legal compliance purposes. You may request account deletion at any time.

9. Children's Privacy

BookedSolid Pro is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes via email. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
