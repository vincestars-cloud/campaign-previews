Roofing Industry Context

The Shared Lead Rat Race

  • Lead platforms send the same lead to up to 16 different roofing contractors
  • Close rate on shared leads: ~13% vs 27% on exclusive
  • Effective CAC through platforms: $1,400+ per booked job (4-5x SEO cost)
  • BBB F rating, 1,900+ complaints in 3 years, FTC $7.2M settlement against major platforms

The Data Quality Crisis

  • “LEADS ARE 95% BAD OR FALSE” — actual contractor reviews
  • 27% wrong task leads, 21% fake contact info
  • “Most jobs are fake or wrong task — customers saying they have a flat roof when they have shingles”
  • Skip trace accuracy: 70-95% meaning up to 30% wrong/disconnected numbers
  • 42% of potential leads missed due to poor data handling

Platform Lock-In & Billing

  • $2,030 early termination penalties
  • Refunds only as platform credit, not cash
  • “Every staff member stated they had no authority to credit the account”
  • Billing continues even with documented bad leads

Target Audience

  • Roofing company owners, ops managers, estimator team leads
  • Pain: shared leads, fake numbers, wrong service area, driving blind without property data
  • 71% of roofers rely on word-of-mouth because lead quality is so bad
