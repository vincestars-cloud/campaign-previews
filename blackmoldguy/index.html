The Silent Health Threat

Mold is more than an eyesore

Long-term mold exposure can cause respiratory problems, chronic fatigue, allergic reactions, and neurological symptoms. Georgia and Florida's humid climates make homes especially vulnerable. If any of these sound familiar, mold could be the cause.

Asthma & Wheezing

Persistent coughing, wheezing, or shortness of breath — especially indoors. Kids and elderly are hit hardest by airborne mold spores.

Itchy, Watery Eyes

Year-round congestion, sneezing, and itchy eyes that no medication can fix. Mold exposure mimics chronic allergies that never resolve.

Fatigue & Brain Fog

Waking up tired, difficulty concentrating, and chronic headaches. Mold toxins accumulate in your body over time, draining your energy daily.

Chronic Sinus Infections

Recurring sinus pressure, congestion, and infections that antibiotics can't seem to clear. Mold is one of the most common hidden triggers.

Persistent Coughing

A cough that lingers for weeks or months, especially when you're at home. If it improves when you leave the house, mold is a likely culprit.

Skin Irritation & Rashes

Unexplained skin rashes, hives, or irritation. Mold spores can trigger immune responses that manifest on your skin, especially in children.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know

How is your approach different from other mold companies?

Most companies treat the symptom — they wipe down what's visible and leave. I take a whole-home, health-first approach: identifying the root cause, eradicating mold from every surface and the air, applying antimicrobial protection, and proving the results with post-treatment lab testing. My goal isn't a cosmetic fix — it's permanent resolution.

Are the chemicals you use safe for my family and pets?

Absolutely. I exclusively use products on the EPA's Safer Chemical List. They're effective enough to destroy 99.9% of mold while being safe for children, pets, and individuals with chemical sensitivities. Your family can return to normal activity shortly after treatment.

How long does the remediation process take?

Most residential projects take 1-3 days depending on size and severity. I'll give you an accurate timeline during the initial inspection. My process is thorough but efficient — I respect your time and minimize disruption to your daily life.

What does the 1-year guarantee cover?

If mold returns in any area I treated within 12 months, I'll come back and re-treat at no cost. This guarantee is possible because my whole-home approach — including antimicrobial barriers — is designed to prevent regrowth, not just remove what's there today.

Do you offer financing or payment plans?

Yes. Your health shouldn't wait because of budget constraints. I offer flexible payment plans to make remediation accessible. We'll discuss options during your free consultation so there are no surprises.

I'm buying a home — should I get a mold inspection first?

100%. Standard home inspections often miss mold issues, especially when sellers have covered them with fresh paint or drywall. I've saved buyers from purchasing homes with massive hidden mold problems. Think of it as insurance for one of the biggest purchases of your life.
