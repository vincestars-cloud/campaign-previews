The Real Problem

You Don't Have a Lead Problem.
You Have a Data Problem.

Every option you've tried is either banned, broken, or burning your margin.

Trigger leads are gone and they're not coming back

The Homebuyers Privacy Protection Act banned trigger lead sales in September 2025. The channel that delivered borrowers the moment they hit the credit bureaus no longer exists. That's not a temporary disruption — it's permanent.

Shared leads cost more than your profit per loan

LendingTree at $50/lead with a 1% conversion rate means $5,000 per funded loan in lead costs alone. The average net profit per loan in 2024 was $443. The math doesn't work. It was never going to work.

"9 out of 10 ghost me" — half your list is disconnected or wrong

Industry-wide, skip-traced lists run 20-40% bad numbers. That's your team dialing dead lines for 2-3 hours every morning before they talk to a real person. Every wrong number is a rep losing confidence and a prospect you'll never reach.

Realtor relationships don't scale — they go cold when you need them most

You built your pipeline on goodwill. Lunches, referrals, relationships. It works until a Realtor switches lenders, goes on vacation, or gets recruited by someone else. Goodwill isn't a pipeline. It's a dependency.

By the time you call, they've already heard from three other LOs

Shared leads go to multiple lenders simultaneously. Rate shopping starts immediately. Your margin disappears in the first 60 seconds. When you call borrowers from your own list — people nobody else has contacted — the conversation is entirely different.

Common Questions

How is this different from LendingTree or shared lead platforms?
Shared lead platforms sell the same lead to 3-5 lenders simultaneously. The borrower gets bombarded with calls immediately and rate shops everyone. BookedSolid Pro gives you raw homeowner records that nobody else has purchased. You build the relationship from scratch — but you're the only one calling. No bidding war. No rate race.
How accurate are the phone numbers?
90% working number accuracy — compared to the industry average of 60-70% for skip-traced lists. Any number that doesn't connect gets replaced free. No limits. Our records come from CoreLogic and ListSource data, not scraped directories or stale aggregator files.
Does this replace trigger leads?
Not identically — trigger leads hit at the exact moment someone applies for a mortgage. BookedSolid Pro lets you find homeowners who are in a refi window based on loan age, equity, and rate environment — before they've applied anywhere. It's proactive rather than reactive, and it's fully legal under the Homebuyers Privacy Protection Act.
Is this TCPA compliant?
DNC status is included with every record so you can filter before you call. We also recommend working with your compliance officer on your calling scripts and consent practices. The data itself is FCRA-compliant sourced from public records and licensed data. With 3,200+ TCPA lawsuits filed in 2025, DNC filtering is non-negotiable — we give you that built-in.
What exactly is the guarantee?
Work your verified list for 5 days. If you don't book 25 qualified mortgage appointments with homeowners in your desired market, all the leads you purchased are free — no questions asked, no conditions, no fight. We make this offer because the data works. Bad numbers also get replaced free with no limits.
What does "you own the list" actually mean?
You download a CSV file. That file is yours. You can load it into any CRM, dialer, or direct mail platform. We don't charge you per call, per use, or per deployment. You bought the records — use them however your compliance requirements allow. No platform lock-in.
