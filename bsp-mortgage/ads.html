Mortgage Industry Context

The Trigger Lead Ban

  • Homebuyers Privacy Protection Act banned trigger leads March 2026
  • Trigger leads were $0.10-$0.50/lead with highest intent — the industry cheat code for 15 years
  • Every LO in America now competing for same shared leads from 3 aggregators at 10x-100x the price

The Math Problem

  • LendingTree: $30-$150/lead, 0.5-2% close rate = $3,000-$15,000 per funded loan
  • Bankrate: $100-$250/lead = $3,750-$6,250 per funded loan
  • MBA data: $11,000-$12,500 to originate one loan, net profit $443
  • TCPA lawsuits up 58% YoY — shared leads create compliance risk

Target Audience

  • Loan officers, branch managers, mortgage ops leads
  • Pain: pipeline collapsed overnight, shared leads hemorrhaging money, reps quitting
