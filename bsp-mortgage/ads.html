Independent MLO/Broker Context (v2 Corrections)

Who This Actually Targets

  • Independent mortgage brokers and self-employed LOs — NOT bank LOs or call center staff
  • 18,223 independent broker businesses in the US (2025). Broker channel at 27.4% market share — 15-year high
  • These people bear ALL costs personally: compliance, technology, leads, insurance, licensing
  • Earn 150–275 bps per loan vs 50–120 bps at a bank — higher upside, zero safety net

Trigger Lead Ban (March 5, 2026) — Corrected Context

  • Homebuyers Privacy Protection Act banned trigger leads. Independents were NOT heavily dependent (<5% of volume)
  • The real impact: it exposed how broken all alternative lead sources already were. Everywhere to go, nothing works.
  • Internet lead costs jumped ~45% YoY as displaced demand floods remaining shared-lead channels
  • Independent brokers celebrated the ban (it stopped clients getting stolen mid-application) but still need a prospecting strategy

Real Pain Points (r/loanoriginators Ground Truth)

  • #1: Dead numbers — lists with 40–70% disconnect rates. “Confirming, one call at a time, that the list is garbage.”
  • #2: Realtor dependency — asymmetric relationship. You close 4 loans. They go with someone who bought them lunch.
  • #3: Shared lead economics — same borrower reached by 5 LOs in 12 minutes. You’re paying full price to be call #4.
  • #4: Pipeline roulette — vendor controls deal flow. Stop paying, pipeline stops. Can’t hire confidently.
  • #5: Call burnout — not from rejection, from dead data. “9/10 don’t pick up.” Daily grind destroys morale.

Target Persona

  • 1–5 person independent brokerage. Self-employed or small team. 2–8 loans/month. Commission-only.
  • Already tried multiple lead sources, got burned. Deeply skeptical. Proof must be concrete and specific.
  • Compliance anxiety is personal — TCPA/RESPA violations land on them individually, not a corporate legal team.
  • Landing page: solidbookedpro.com/mortgage
