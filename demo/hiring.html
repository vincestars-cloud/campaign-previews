Job Description

Coastal Shield Roofing is looking for motivated door-to-door sales reps in the Miami-Dade area. No roofing experience needed — we train you. You need energy, a good attitude, and the ability to knock 80+ doors a day.

What you'll do:

  • Knock doors in assigned neighborhoods after storms or in target areas
  • Talk to homeowners about free roof inspections
  • Set appointments for our inspection crew
  • Follow up with interested homeowners via text

What we're looking for:

  • Reliable transportation (you'll drive to neighborhoods)
  • Comfortable talking to strangers at their door
  • Available to work at least 5 days a week
  • Clean background check

Pay: Commission-based. Top reps earn $1,500-$3,000/week. We provide leads, training, branded materials, and storm alerts so you know exactly where to knock.

Phone Screen Questions
  1. Why are you interested in door-to-door sales?
  2. Have you done any canvassing or sales before? If so, where?
  3. How many doors can you knock in a day?
  4. Are you comfortable with commission-only pay?
  5. Do you have reliable transportation?

Job Description

Coastal Shield Roofing needs canvassers to walk neighborhoods and leave door hangers, flyers, and info packets for homeowners. This is not sales — you don't need to pitch anything. Just get our materials on doors in the right areas.

What you'll do:

  • Walk assigned neighborhoods and hang materials on doors
  • Take photos of damaged roofs you spot (we'll train you on what to look for)
  • Log which streets you covered in a simple app

Requirements:

  • Reliable transportation
  • Able to walk 3-5 miles per shift
  • Clean background
  • Part-time or full-time — flexible

Pay: $15/hour + bonus per appointment that comes from your area. Most canvassers earn $18-$22/hour after bonuses.
