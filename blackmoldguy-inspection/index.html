FAQ

The questions skeptics ask — before they become believers

Is the inspection really free?

Yes — no credit card, no obligation. I come out, perform a full 18-point assessment, and give you a clear picture of what's going on. You decide what to do next. No pressure, ever.

What does the 1-year guarantee cover?

If mold returns in any area I treated within 12 months, I'll come back and re-treat at no cost. This is possible because the whole-home approach — including antimicrobial barriers — is designed to prevent regrowth, not just remove what's visible today.

What does the 18-point inspection cover?

Visual inspection of all high-risk zones (bathroom, kitchen, basement, HVAC/ducts, crawlspace, attic), moisture readings throughout the property, air quality evaluation, and a written summary with photos. You leave with documentation — not a guess.

How is your approach different from other mold companies?

Most companies treat the symptom — they wipe down what's visible and leave. I specialize in mold-sensitive individuals and take a holistic approach: consultation, preparation, restoration, and lab verification. IICRC S520 certified with a focus on mycotoxins and ultrafine particles — not just surface mold.

Are the products safe for my family and pets?

Absolutely. I exclusively use products on the EPA's Safer Chemical List. They're effective enough to destroy 99.9% of mold while being safe for children, pets, and individuals with chemical sensitivities. No harsh chemical smells, no evacuation required for days.

How quickly can you come out?

For urgent cases where someone is experiencing health symptoms, I prioritize same-day or next-day visits. Fill out the form and mark "ASAP" — I'll reach out within hours, not days.
