Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before claiming your market.

How is this different from every lead vendor I've tried? ⌄

Three things: (1) Your leads are at cost — you pay the platforms directly, we don't mark up a penny. (2) Our USA appointment setters call, qualify on three gates (assets, timeline, intent), and book directly to your calendar. (3) You get every call recording. Listen yourself. No black box. And your prospects have consumed 10-15 pieces of your branded content before anyone calls them — they know your name before the conversation starts.

What does "leads at cost" actually mean? ⌄

It means you pay the ad platforms (Facebook, Google, YouTube) directly for your lead generation. We manage the campaigns, but your ad dollars go straight to the platform — not through us. If a lead costs $15 to generate, you pay $15. Not $250 like the lead vendors charge. We make money from the service fee, not by marking up your leads. That's how we stay aligned with your success.

I already have an IMO/FMO. Does this conflict? ⌄

Not at all. We're not an IMO. We don't touch your commissions, your carriers, or your contracts. We only handle client acquisition — generating leads, qualifying them, and booking them to your calendar. Think of us as your marketing and appointment setting department. Keep your IMO. Keep your carriers. Keep your overrides.

How do you qualify the leads? ⌄

Three gates, no exceptions. (1) Assets: $100K+ in investable retirement savings — 401k, IRA, pension, or other investments. (2) Timeline: Within 2 years of retirement, or recently retired. (3) Intent: Actively interested in retirement income vehicles, protection strategies, or rollover options. If they don't pass all three, they don't get booked.

What about compliance? ⌄

Every lead has 1:1 documented consent — fully auditable. Every piece of content can be reviewed before it goes live. We don't make claims about returns, guarantees, or specific products in any marketing material. We've built this system for the most compliance-sensitive industry in America.

Why USA appointment setters? Why does it matter? ⌄

Your prospects are 60-70 year old retirees considering what to do with their life savings. They deserve a real, warm conversation with someone who understands retirement planning — not an overseas call center reading a script. Our setters are trained specifically on retirement planning conversations. Listen to the call recordings yourself — you'll hear the difference immediately.

What CRMs do you integrate with? ⌄

Redtail, Wealthbox, Salesforce, and most major CRMs used by financial advisors. Every qualified lead — including full contact info, qualification notes, and call recording — syncs automatically. No manual data entry.

What if I want more than 5-8 appointments per week? ⌄

Scale up anytime. The system is built to grow with your practice. Want 10-15 appointments per week? We increase your campaign volume and our setters handle the additional flow. You control the pace.

How quickly can I get started? ⌄

First qualified appointments within 14 days of setup. We build your branded video funnel, launch your campaigns, and our setters start qualifying and booking within the first two weeks. By week three, you should be seeing a consistent flow of 5-8 qualified appointments per week.
