About The Role

Coastal Shield Roofing is looking for motivated Door-to-Door Sales Representatives to join our growing team in Miami-Dade County. You'll be the first point of contact for homeowners, knocking on doors in assigned neighborhoods, talking to people about free roof inspections, and setting appointments for our inspection crew.

This isn't cold selling — you're offering a free service that protects their home. After storms, homeowners are actively looking for help, and our system tells you exactly which neighborhoods to hit.

What You'll Do
  • Knock 60-100 doors per day in assigned neighborhoods
  • Talk to homeowners about free roof inspections and storm damage
  • Set appointments for our inspection crew using a simple mobile app
  • Take photos of visible roof damage and log addresses
  • Follow up with interested homeowners who weren't home
  • Report daily activity and results to your team lead
Requirements
  • Reliable personal vehicle (mileage reimbursed)
  • Comfortable talking to strangers and handling rejection
  • Available Monday through Friday, rain or shine
  • Smartphone with data plan
  • Clean background check
  • Sales or canvassing experience preferred but not required — we train
Compensation

100% Commission-Based. You eat what you kill. Top reps consistently earn $1,500 - $3,500/week. Our best rep made $4,200 last week during the storm surge.

We provide: training, storm alerts so you know where to go, lead lists, branded materials, and a dedicated inspection crew so you never have to worry about the technical side.

Bonus structure: $50 per booked inspection that results in a signed contract. Average close rate is 35%, so if you book 20 inspections/week, that's ~7 closes = $350 in bonuses on top of your commission.

About The Role

We need reliable Canvassers to walk neighborhoods and leave door hangers on homes in targeted areas. You don't need to sell anything — just walk, hang materials, and take photos of any visible roof damage you spot. Great for people who like being active and working independently.

What You'll Do
  • Walk assigned neighborhoods and hang branded door materials
  • Take photos of roofs with visible damage (cracked tiles, missing shingles, tarps)
  • Log streets covered and photos taken in a simple app
  • Cover 3-5 miles per shift (4-6 hour shifts)
  • Work independently — you pick your shift times within your assigned area
Requirements
  • Reliable transportation to get to starting locations
  • Comfortable walking 3-5 miles per shift in Florida weather
  • Smartphone with camera
  • Clean background check
  • Part-time (15-25 hrs/week) or full-time (35+ hrs/week) available
Compensation

$15/hour base + $25 bonus per appointment that comes from your area. Most canvassers earn $18-$24/hour after bonuses.

Weekly pay via direct deposit. We provide all door hanger materials and training on what to photograph.
