Our Story

If your skin tells a story too,
you're not alone.

When my son started having eczema flare-ups, I searched everywhere for something I could trust on his skin. Something gentle. Something effective. Something honest. Everything I found was too harsh, too synthetic, or too far removed from what the body actually needs.

So I started making it myself. Through deep research, careful experimentation, and a profound respect for what plants can do that chemicals can't, I developed formulations that brought real relief to his skin. What began as a mother's instinct to protect her child became something much larger.

Because I carried my own story too. Burn marks from long nights as a baking entrepreneur — working ovens hotter than I should have trusted. Sunspots from lying on the beach in St. Martin, where I lived for three years. And acne scars — years of cystic acne and hormonal shifts, my body constantly trying to find balance.

For a long time I saw these marks as flaws. But eventually I understood something different. My skin was responding to my life. And in that understanding, I found a calling: create care that feels intuitive, for everyone.

"I make products that allow you to indulge in a truly authentic experience — a journey that is rooted in goodness and ideal for your well-being."

Oma Organics was created with that understanding. It wasn't born from trends. It wasn't born from perfection. It was born from listening — to my body, to my son's, to the stories written on our skin. Everything is made by hand, in small batches, right here in Atlanta. When one piece of the process slows down, everything waits its turn. We don't substitute. We don't rush. We don't cut corners.

Founded in 2020. "Oma" means "Good" in Igbo. Not louder. Not trend-driven. Not performative. Good means care that works with your body, scent that feels personal, products that earn their place. No matter what you order, no matter how it arrives — you can always expect goodness. In the ingredients, in the intention, and in the experience. That's just how we do things at Oma.

Frequently Asked

Everything you might want to know.
What is Oma Organics?+
Oma Organics is a bodycare company that specializes in creating natural and organic non-comedogenic body oils, body balms, shower gels, soy wax candles with essential oil fragrance blends, and odor-eliminating room sprays. Our products are made with high-quality ingredients to provide nourishment and a luxurious experience for your body and home.
Are your products 100% natural and organic?+
Yes — we take pride in using only natural and organic ingredients in our body and skincare products. We prioritize sourcing high-quality, plant-based ingredients that are free from harmful chemicals, synthetic fragrances, and artificial preservatives. Our commitment to natural and organic formulations ensures that you can trust the purity and effectiveness of our products.
What is a non-comedogenic body oil?+
A non-comedogenic body oil is an oil-based product that is specially formulated not to clog pores or cause acne breakouts. Oma Organics' non-comedogenic body oils are carefully crafted using lightweight and fast-absorbing oils that provide deep hydration without leaving a greasy residue. They are suitable for all skin types, including sensitive and acne-prone skin.
Can I use the body balms on my face?+
While our body balms are designed primarily for body use, they can also be used on the face, especially for areas that need intensive moisturization. However, it's essential to keep in mind that everyone's skin is unique, and certain facial areas may have different sensitivities. We recommend patch testing before applying the body balm to your face to ensure compatibility with your skin.
Are your shower gels suitable for sensitive skin?+
Yes, our shower gels are formulated with gentle, plant-based cleansers and nourishing ingredients, making them suitable for sensitive skin. We avoid harsh sulfates and artificial fragrances that can irritate the skin. However, as with any new product, it's always a good idea to perform a patch test on a small area of your skin to ensure compatibility.
Are your body mists safe for daily use?+
Absolutely! Our body mists are designed to provide a refreshing burst of fragrance for your body throughout the day. They are made with natural essential oils and botanical extracts, ensuring a delightful scent without the use of synthetic fragrances. Feel free to use them as often as desired, but discontinue use if any irritation occurs.
Do your soy wax candles emit harmful toxins when burned?+
No. Our soy wax candles are crafted from 100% natural soy wax, which burns cleanly and does not emit harmful toxins or soot when properly used. We use natural wooden wicks and lead-free cotton wicks and essential oil and fragrance oil blends, ensuring a safe and healthy candle-burning experience for you and your environment.
Are your products cruelty-free and vegan?+
We are proud to say that all Oma Organics products are cruelty-free and vegan friendly. We do not test our products on animals, and we do not use animal-derived ingredients, with the exception of our cruelty free beeswax in our balms. Our commitment to ethical practices extends to every aspect of our brand, ensuring that our products are both effective and compassionate.
Can I use your products if I have allergies or sensitivities?+
While we strive to create products that are gentle and suitable for a wide range of individuals, we understand that everyone's skin is unique, and allergies or sensitivities may vary. We recommend carefully reviewing the ingredient list of each product before use. If you have known allergies or sensitivities, we suggest performing a patch test on a small area of skin and monitoring for any adverse reactions. If you have specific concerns, it's always a good idea to consult with a healthcare professional.
Where can I purchase Oma Organics products?+
You can purchase Oma Organics products directly from our official website at www.omaorganics.com. We offer a convenient online shopping experience, and our website provides detailed information about each product, including ingredients and usage directions. Additionally, we may have authorized retailers or stockists where our products are available.
Do you offer international shipping?+
We currently only ship within the United States and hope to add international shipping to our site in the near future. Please watch our site for any updates to our shipping options.
What is your return and refund policy?+
We want you to be completely satisfied with your Oma Organics purchase. If you are not satisfied or if you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact our customer service within 14 days of receiving your order. We will be happy to assist you and provide options to resolve your issue. For detailed information on our return and refund policy, please refer to our Shipping & Returns page.

Shipping & Returns

We want every order to feel right.

Shipping

All orders ship from Atlanta, Georgia. We currently ship within the United States only. Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days. Because everything is made in small batches, some items may take slightly longer during high-demand periods.

Returns

We have a 30-day return policy, which means you have 30 days after receiving your item to request a return. To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it — unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging. You'll also need the receipt or proof of purchase.

To start a return, you can contact us at omaskinfood@gmail.com. Please note that returns will need to be sent to our Atlanta address. If your return is accepted, we'll send you a return shipping label, as well as instructions on how and where to send your package.

Damages & Issues

Please inspect your order upon reception and contact us immediately if the item is defective, damaged, or if you receive the wrong item, so that we can evaluate the issue and make it right.

Exceptions

Certain types of items cannot be returned, like perishable goods, custom products, and personal care goods. We also do not accept returns for hazardous materials, flammable liquids, or gases. Please get in touch if you have questions about your specific item. We cannot accept returns on sale items or gift cards.

Exchanges

The fastest way to ensure you get what you want is to return the item you have, and once the return is accepted, make a separate purchase for the new item.

Refunds

We will notify you once we've received and inspected your return, and let you know if the refund was approved or not. If approved, you'll be automatically refunded on your original payment method within 10 business days. Please remember it can take some time for your bank or credit card company to process and post the refund. If more than 15 business days have passed since we've approved your return, please contact us at omaskinfood@gmail.com.

Sharing Your Information
