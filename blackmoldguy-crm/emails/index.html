Welcome

Fires the second a lead opts in. Establishes Deonco's voice, lowers the bar to reply, lists 3 questions that pre-qualify the call.

No-Sale Follow-up Sequence (5 emails)

Triggered when Deonco moves a consult to Follow-up. Spaced over 12 days. Addresses the exact objections that come up after a consult — why DIY doesn't work, what to ask other mold companies, what's actually in the products, real case study, why this is personal for Deonco.

Hormozi / Cory McCarthy Reactivation (6 emails)

Manual trigger for past customers + cold leads. Built around the "I owe you a free re-check, my mistake" frame from Hormozi's session with Cory McCarthy — high-perceived-value reactivation that doesn't feel like marketing.

Review Flow (3 emails)

Triggered the moment Deonco clicks "Mark complete" on a job. Customer rates 1-5 stars. 5+ are auto-routed to Google. Under 4 → stays internal, Deonco gets a private alert.
